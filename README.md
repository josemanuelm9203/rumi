## ⚙️ Running your first task

Rumi runs in your terminal. You do not need to be a coder to use it. Open your terminal by pressing the Windows key. Type "cmd" and press Enter.

Once the window opens, type:
rumi --help

The system will show a menu of choices. You can pick an option related to hypothesis discovery. The system will ask you for a topic. Type your research topic and press Enter. Rumi begins the ten-stage pipeline. It breaks down your topic into smaller parts. It extracts entities and builds a map of the information. The system then looks for logical traps in your data. It finishes by suggesting new ways to test your hypothesis.

## ✨ Main features

The software focuses on four areas of discovery:

*   **Hypothesis Generation:** The system takes your initial idea and shifts it into a testable format. It creates detailed steps for your next experiment.
*   **Knowledge Mapping:** Rumi connects data points into a visual graph. This helps you spot links between facts.
*   **Contradiction Mining:** The system flags parts of your research that do not align with verified data. This saves time on manual review.
*   **Skeptic Review:** A specific module acts as a critic. It challenges your assumptions to ensure your research stays strong.
